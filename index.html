Welcome to a version of Wordle for The Burnt City cast list!

Our beloved show may have come to an end but that doesn't mean we can't keep its memory alive 💚

Peeple will now grab an old cast list from the community-maintained archive (many thanks to those who have maintained it over course of the show!) every day at midnight.

The aim is to guess the cast. The page will alphabetically list all the performers definitely in that day's cast at the top, and all characters definitely in that day's show down the side.

For each character, select the performer who you think is playing them. Once a performer has been selected, they will disappear from the list at the top. Any repeats will be highlighted in red.

Once you have made a guess for every character, with no repeats, you'll be able to hit Guess!

Correct guesses will be locked in green, incorrect guesses will be in orange. All your guesses will be recorded in columns to the right so you can keep track of what you've tried. There's no limit to how many times you can guess.

Good luck!
